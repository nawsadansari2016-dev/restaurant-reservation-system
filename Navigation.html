Welcome to FoodMandu – A Taste of Perfection!

At FoodMandu, we believe food is more than just a meal—it’s an experience.

Our carefully curated menu blends traditional recipes with modern culinary artistry.

Our Menu

Starters

Begin your meal with our selection of exquisite starters. Our French Onion Soup features slow-cooked caramelized onions in a rich beef broth, topped with a crispy layer of melted Gruyère cheese. Try our Bruschetta, a delightful combination of toasted baguette slices topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. For a warm and savory option, enjoy our Stuffed Mushrooms, filled with a rich blend of cheese, garlic, and herbs.

Main Course

Indulge in our expertly crafted main courses. Our Grilled Salmon is perfectly cooked and served with a side of buttery asparagus and a lemon-dill sauce. Enjoy the rich and hearty flavors of our Beef Bourguignon, a slow-braised beef stew cooked with red wine, mushrooms, and pearl onions, served over creamy mashed potatoes. For a vegetarian delight, our Creamy Risotto is made with Arborio rice, Parmesan cheese, and a touch of truffle oil for a luxurious finish.

Desserts

Complete your meal with one of our decadent desserts. Our Chocolate Lava Cake offers a molten chocolate center encased in a delicate cake, served with a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream. The classic Tiramisu layers espresso-soaked ladyfingers with a creamy mascarpone filling and is dusted with cocoa powder for the perfect balance of sweetness and depth. For a refined treat, enjoy our Crème Brûlée, with its silky custard base and caramelized sugar topping that crackles with each spoonful.
